This book collects twelve flower photographs that were circulating on the internet (mainly Pinterest and Instagram) and, with the assistance of Adobe Illustrator‚Äôs automated Image Trace function (with slight modifications), transforms them into twelve flower silhouettes.

1. Twelve Flower Pictures
I collected random flower pictures from the internet. (These appear on the book's back cover.)


2. Twelve Silhouettes
The previously collected bitmap images are converted into vector graphic silhouettes using Image Trace ‚Äî with my intention somewhat intervening in the parameter values ‚Äî according to specified criteria. The twelve silhouettes are placed on the 114 x 164 mm format base and tagged from (a) to (l).


3. Combinations of the Twelve Silhouettes
The twelve silhouettes are grouped into sets of three. Three patterns are applied to reveal overlapping areas between silhouettes, mechanically combining them within a predefined layout. This process generates a total of 220 combined result images.
