This book collects twelve flower photographs that once circulated on the internet (mainly Pinterest and Instagram), and—using the automated Image Trace function of Adobe CC Illustrator 2025 (with slight modifications)—transforms them into twelve flower silhouettes. These silhouettes are arranged on a 00x00 format page, organized into layouts, and assigned patterns that divide them into three layers to show overlaps. Through mechanically simple combinations, they generate 2xx composite graphic images. The book documents both the process and the results of this production.
